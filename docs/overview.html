Introduction to Statistics & Data Science

Chapter 1 Overview

An overview of the preparation course.

This online course allows you to have a peek inside the Statistics & Data Science master programme. This way you can judge more easily whether the programme is something for you, and whether you are sufficiently confident in your mathematics skills. You can also use this online course to prepare for the first semester.

Please note that the online course is neither a requirement for, nor a guarantee of admission to the master programme. For the actual requirements and other information regarding the master programme, always consult the official Leiden University websites.
